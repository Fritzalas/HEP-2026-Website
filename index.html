Welcome

The HEP 2026 Conference is organised by the Hellenic Society for the Study of High Energy Physics (HSSHEP) and hosted by the National Technical University of Athens, 8th–11th July 2026.

Founded in 1975, HSSHEP brings together the majority of Greek scientists working in High Energy Physics, both in Greece and abroad. The Society promotes scientific research in the field and contributes to informing the public and the Greek state on matters related to High Energy Physics and Cosmology.

The annual conference provides a platform for presenting recent experimental and theoretical developments. Young researchers are especially encouraged to participate, while distinguished international colleagues are invited to present cutting-edge topics.
