Hotels & Accommodation

Participants may consider staying in the areas of Ampelokipoi, Panormou, Ilisia, Megaro Mousikis and Michalakopoulou Avenue. These areas offer convenient access to Zografou and the National Technical University of Athens.

Recommended areas

The hotels below are large, established city hotels in areas with good public transport links and reasonable access to Zografou, without being in the most expensive parts of Athens.

Ampelokipoi / Panormou

President Hotel

A large 4-star hotel on Kifisias Avenue, close to Panormou and Ampelokipoi metro stations.
