Climate Overview

Athens has a typical Mediterranean climate, characterised by hot, dry summers and mild winters.

In July, average daytime temperatures are around 30°C, while evening temperatures are typically 22–24°C.

Rainfall is very limited during this period. Participants are advised to bring light summer clothing, sunscreen, and a light jacket for cooler evenings.
